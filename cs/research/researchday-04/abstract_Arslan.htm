A Recent Work on the Constrained Longest Common Subsequence Problems

 

Abdullah N. Arslan

 

Abstract

Given strings S1, S2, and P, the constrained longest subsequence problem is to find the longest common subsequence lcs of S1 and S2 such that this lcs contains P as a subsequence. In a recent work we have improved the time complexity of the problem significantly. This paper is a good representative of our recent research.
