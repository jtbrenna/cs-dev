Metric Space Embeddings in Data Stream Algorithms

S. Muthukrishnan
Rutgers University and AT&T Research

ABSTRACT

There is a rich theory of metric space embeddings; these methods are now being applied to algorithms that deal with super-fast data streams. I will describe such applications in spaces that deal with vectors, strings and trees. These algorithms have applications in database systems, networking and computational biology.

Bio

S. (Muthu) Muthukrishnan graduated from the Courant Institute of Mathematical Sciences, NYU, with PhD work in pattern matching algorithms and probabilistic games. Since then he has been at U. Warwick, UK, Bell Labs, AT&T Research and Rutgers Univ. His research interest is generally in Algorithms applied to Databases, Networking, Wireless Systems, Scheduling and Computational Biology. His current interest is in massive data set analysis and data streaming algorithms. See webpage http://www.cs.rutgers.edu/~muthu for more details.
