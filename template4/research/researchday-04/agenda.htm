University of Vermont

Friday August 27, 2004

 

The main focus this year will be on the research activities of “our” graduate faculty. Individual graduate faculty members of the department and their research advisees will provide overviews of their research activities and ongoing research projects. We hope this event will lead to opportunities for students to identify research advisors and for colleagues to identify research collaborators.
