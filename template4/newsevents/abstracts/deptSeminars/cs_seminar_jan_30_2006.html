How to Write and Publish Top-Quality Research Papers

Xindong Wu

Department of Computer Science
The University of Vermont

Date: Monday January 30, 2006
Time: 12:20 p.m. - 1:20 p.m.
Location: 367 Votey

Abstract

This talk will start with the main areas and major forums in knowledge and data engineering including data mining, and cover key issues in writing top-quality papers for first-rate journals and conferences. How to present a convincing case with a well-written introduction and in-depth empirical results, and how to deal with reviewing feedback, are discussed with real-life experiences.
